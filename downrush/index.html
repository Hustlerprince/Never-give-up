DOWNRUSH

Downrush is a cross between Tetris and Scrabble. Use the left and right arrow keys (or the buttons below) to play.

Make words either across or down. The letters are worth the same points as in Scrabble, for example, E is 1, B is 3, and Z is 10.

This little game was made by Ben Hoyt for his daughter's eighth birthday.
